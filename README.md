# r04

## Research title

Digital Transformation and AI Application in Early Childhood Education in Vietnam: Awareness, Readiness, and Barriers

## Overview

This repository contains the materials and metadata for a research paper presented in a national scientific conference proceeding. The study examines how early childhood education in Vietnam is adapting to digital transformation and artificial intelligence through teacher awareness, readiness, and implementation barriers.

## Research summary

- Research design: descriptive survey research using data collected from 426 early childhood education teachers.
- Main objective: assess current awareness and readiness of teachers for digital technology and AI in the classroom, and identify key obstacles that limit practical adoption.
- Key finding: teachers reported a readiness score of 4.04 out of 5, but significant implementation barriers remain, including lack of digital skills (71.6%) and insufficient infrastructure.
