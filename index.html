Digital Transformation and AI in Early Childhood Education

Research overview and access portal for the Vietnam early childhood education study.

About this research

This project explores how digital transformation and artificial intelligence are being applied in early childhood education in Vietnam.

The research is based on survey responses from 426 teachers and evaluates awareness, readiness, and practical barriers to implementation in classroom settings.

Key details

Title

Author

Affiliation

Vietnamese (primary), English (abstract and keywords)
Document type
National Scientific Conference Proceeding Article
Abstract
This study analyzes the current state of digital transformation and AI application in early childhood education in Vietnam using descriptive statistics. It identifies readiness levels and key barriers such as technology skills gaps and infrastructure limitations.

Guest reading guide

The embedded presentation is organized into logical sections. Use this guide to review the study step by step.

  1. Introduction — understand the study goal, context, and the importance of digital transformation in early childhood education.
  2. Research objectives — note the questions about teacher awareness, readiness, and technology barriers.
  3. Methodology — check how the survey was conducted, who participated, and how the data were analyzed.
  4. Results — focus on readiness scores, awareness levels, and the most significant barriers reported by teachers.
  5. Discussion — review the practical implications for classrooms and recommendations for improving digital competence.
  6. Conclusion — summarize the main findings and the next steps for research or practice.
